Why I Love Tech

Technology is a significant part of my life and has contributed to some of my happiest experiences.

From jailbreaking iPhones as a kid - to assembling my first computer - to creating my first computer program - I am always searching to learn something new and interesting in the world of tech. What excites me most about tech is how quickly it evolves, offering endless opportunities to grow, solve problems, and create. It’s not just a career for me—it’s a passion that constantly inspires and challenges me.

However, I’m also someone who has other interests.

Music

When I’m not coding, you’ll likely find me immersed in music - whether it's playing an instrument or discovering new sounds.

In High School, I played the trombone in Marching band and Jazz band. I enjoyed the band community, and made some of my closest friends through it.

After High School, I continuted to create music, shifting more to digital composition and production. I continued to make new friends doing so, and having people to enjoy this process with fueled my enjoyement of the hobby.

Currently, I'm learning how to play the bass guitar. It's the first physical instrument I have played in quite some time, but the experience of learning something new is always a blast!

Outdoors

I'm working at my computer a lot, but I also love spending time outdoors - whether surfing, playing basketball, or just soaking up the sun. I believe that having balance is the key to creativity and productivity.

Getting outside is incredibly important, especially to people involved in tech. It is easy to get wrapped up in a project and forget to take the necessary time to step outside and away from the screens.
